For Every Trojan

The story behind Trojan City Limits

The idea was simple: what if we made a shirt that represented everyone at Anderson High School?

Inspired by Austin City Limits' iconic festival posters - where bands are stacked in that unmistakable lineup format - we created Trojan City Limits. But instead of headliners and opening acts, we feature something better: every single team and club that makes Anderson what it is.

"Because being a Trojan isn't about which group you're in. It's about being part of something bigger."

From Football to French Club. From Cheer to Chess Club. From Debate to DECA. Over 100 groups are represented on the back of this shirt - and that's the whole point.

We believe school spirit shouldn't just belong to the biggest teams or the loudest groups. It belongs to everyone who walks the halls of Anderson. The coders. The artists. The debaters. The athletes. The musicians. The volunteers. All of us.

This is our lineup. This is Anderson. This is Trojan City Limits.
